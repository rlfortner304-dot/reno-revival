# Reno Revival Stability Project

The Reno Revival Stability Project is a community-based initiative operating under the 501(c)(3) umbrella of Reno Revival Church.

Our mission is to support individuals and families facing housing instability, reentry challenges, and life transitions by providing faith-based support, practical resources, and pathways toward long-term stability.

## Programs (In Development)
- Housing stabilization support
- Reentry and life-skills mentoring
- Community outreach and advocacy
- Faith-centered recovery and support services

## Organizational Structure
Reno Revival Stability Project operates as a program of Reno Revival Church (501(c)(3)) while building capacity for future independent operations.
